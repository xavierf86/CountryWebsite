Famous Landmarks in Chile:

Easter Island- Moai Statues

Huge stone statues made by the Rapa Nui people. They are one of Chile's most famous symbols and show the island's ancient culture.

Torres del Paine National Park

Located in Patagonia, this park is known for its mountains, blue lakes, glaciers, and wildlife. It's one of the worlds most beautiful nature destinations.

Atacama Desert

One of the driest desert on Earth. It has places like Valle de la Luna, geysers, salt flats, and amazing night skies.

La Moneda Palace

The presidential palace in Santiago. Home for the Chilean President and it's government. It's an important building and the place where many major political events happened.

San Cristobal Hill

A big hill in Santiago with a giant statue of the Virgin Mary on top. Considered to give one of the best views of the whole city.
