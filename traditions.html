Chile: Traditions

Chile History Traditions Culture Landmarks

Traditional Dances

Cueca is the Chilean national dance. It is performed by hankerchiefs, and traditional music. It is danced everywhere during Fiestas Patrias in september, specifically on September 18. This dance is an important part of Chilean culture.

Traditional Foods

Chile has many traditional foods that people enjoy during Holidays and family gatherings. Empanandas stuffed with beef and onions are the most famous dish. Another dish that is well known, called Pastel de Choclo, is a pie that has sweet corn, which is layered with meat and eggs. These foods are a big part of Chile's culture and bring families together.

Fiesta Patrias

Fiestas Patrias is one of the biggest celebrations in Chile, held every year on september 18 and 19. Families enjoy food, games, rodeos, reunions, and cueca dance. People decorste with flags, eat empanadas, and some can gather in community festivals called ramadas. Fiestas Patrias is one of the most important traditions in the country.

Rodeo Chileno

Rodeo Chileno is one of Chile's most traditional sports. It has been practiced since the 1800s, and is considered part of the countrys national image. In Chilean rodeo, two riders, called huasos, work together to guide a calf into another area. The riders usually wear traditional clothing like ponchos, hats, and boots. Chilean rodeo usually happen during the Independence Day celebrations, also known as Fiesta Patrias. Chilean rodeo event shows the strong connection Chileans have with rural life.

Ramadas/Fondas

Ramadas and fondas are temporary festival areas built during Chile's Independence Day celebrations in September. Ramadas and fondas have music, food, games, and dancing. People come to eat traditional foods like empanadas, Pastel de Choclo, and different desserts. Families gather to dance Cueca, and to enjoy decorations with Chilean flags and their colors. These celebrations show the cultral values and traditions that are important to Chileans and their country.
